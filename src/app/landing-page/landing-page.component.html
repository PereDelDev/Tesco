About Us

Founded in 1996, TESCO has evolved into a leading contractor, offering a wide range of services including machining, fabrication, welding, and tool rentals. From humble beginnings as a single shop, we’ve expanded to three locations, serving diverse industries such as energy, petroleum, geothermal, oil & gas, manufacturing, and mining.

Over the years, we’ve built strategic partnerships with select international companies, bringing cutting-edge technologies and innovations in both product development and service delivery to better meet the needs of our clients.
